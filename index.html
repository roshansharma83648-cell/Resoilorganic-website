About Resoil Organics

Our mission is simple: convert waste to wealth and make healthy soil affordable for every farmer.

From Gurgaon, for Indian farms

Resoil Organics was started by a student-led team in Gurugram with one belief: soil is a living engine, not just dirt. We saw farmers spending heavily on chemicals while their soil quality kept falling. So we designed a lean, local vermicomposting model that turns cow dung and organic waste into powerful plant food.

Every kilogram of Resoil vermicompost means fewer chemicals on fields, richer soil life, and safer food for families. We focus on transparency, lab testing, and farmer education so that our impact goes beyond one-time sales.
